Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is known for its diverse ecosystems, abundant water, and life-sustaining atmosphere. Often called the Blue Planet, it has a unique environment that supports a wide variety of life forms.

Earth Stats

Feature Value
Distance from Sun 149.6 million km
Diameter 12,742 km
Mass 5.97 × 1024 kg
Surface Temperature 14 °C (average)
Length of Day 24 hours
Length of Year 365.25 days
Number of Moons 1

Interesting Facts About Earth

  • Earth has a dynamic atmosphere composed primarily of nitrogen and oxygen, with clouds of water vapor.
  • It has a wide range of climates, from tropical rainforests to polar ice caps.
  • The surface pressure on Earth is 1 atm, which is comfortable for human life.
  • Earth rotates on its axis in the same direction as most other planets in the solar system.

Exploration of Earth

Various space missions have mapped Earth's surface, providing valuable data on its geology, climate, and ecosystems.

Satellites and rovers have been used to study Earth's environment and monitor changes over time.
